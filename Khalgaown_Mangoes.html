About Kahalgaon's Mangoes

Kahalgaon, located in the Bhagalpur district of Bihar, is renowned for its luscious mangoes. The region produces premium varieties known for their taste, aroma, and juiciness.

Famous Mango Varieties

Langra

Known for its rich flavor and smooth texture, Langra is the pride of Bihar’s mango orchards.

Bombaiya

Juicy and delicious, Bombaiya mangoes are a local favorite in Kahalgaon.

Malda

Sweet and fiberless, Malda mangoes are exported across India and loved by mango enthusiasts.

Dussehri

Dussehri mangoes are famous for their pleasant aroma and sweet taste, enjoyed during the summer.
